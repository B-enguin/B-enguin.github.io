Financial sentiment analysis through news and social media

Description

I am currently undertaking this as a personal passion project so as to further my understanding and broaden my knowledge. The aim for this project is to explore how to effectively extract information, specifically general sentiment about a specified product, company or organisation. 

As the world becomes ever more connected, the amount of information that is accessible is also increasing. More often than not, the amount of information available is too substantial for a person to view and understand  without spending a sizable amount of time.  Therefore, there is a need to make information more concise or more easily readable. 

However with recent developments in both computing and machine learning. Many advancements have been made. Thus, in this project, the use of various Natural Language Processing techniques was explored. Particularly in the field of Named Entity Recognition and Sentiment Analysis. So as to be able to automatically label as well as analyse financial news articles and social media posts.
